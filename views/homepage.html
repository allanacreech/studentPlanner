About PlanTastic

You have heard the saying that “Time is money”, well that phrase is true now more than ever. Unfortunately we don’t always use our time as wisely as we think we do. That is where PlanTastic comes in. This site will enable you to track your daily activities and see at a quick glance where you spend the bulk of your time. Thus allowing you to make adjustments to your daily routine to help you maxamize your time and thus be able to be more efficient and make more MONEY!!!

Calendar

In the Calendar you will enter your daily activities. Simply click and drag to select a block of time. Then indicate what 'type' of activity you will be doing (i.e. Work, Eat, Play, Class, Sleep). This information will be stored under your user login and can be accessed and updated anytime.

Time Tracker Chart

The Time Tracker Chart will provide you a break down of how much time you are spending doing the various activities (i.e. Work, Eat, Play, Class, Sleep).
